TweetStats


Please select a query and time-period to perform the analysis:



TweetStats tracks the popularity score of certain popular terms on twitter over time. This feature is for long-term sentiment analysis, like when following political campaigns or analyzing the effects of changes in public sentiments with respect ceratain events.

Note: Currently only a handful terms of our choice have been selected for this feature, and users are generally not given the option to follow their custom queries. However if you have any particular interesting term for long-term analysis in mind, do share with us and we will consider inputting it into our system!
