TweetMood is a free online Twitter Sentiment Analysis Application. It works by acquiring tweets matching to a certain search term that you provide (the same which google works) and modelling the public mood on that search term from those tweets.

There are three options for analyzing Twitter sentiment:
- TweetScore
- TweetCompare
- TweetStats

TweetScore displays the popularity score of a search term of your choice from positive 100 to negative 100. Interested in finding out how popular is something on twitter? TweetScore is the thing you need!

TweetCompare is for ranking two or three search terms of your choice according to their popularity scores. Perform popularity comparisons on politicians, movies, brands, celebrities right now using TweetCompare!

TweetStats tracks the popularity score of certain popular terms on twitter over time. TweetStats is for you if you like following public mood, political campaigns, product marketting campaigns and stock markets!
