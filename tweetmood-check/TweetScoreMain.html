TweetScore displays the popularity score of a search term of your choice from positive 100 to negative 100, using our state-of-the-art algorithm. A score near postive 100 signifies your search query is highly famous on Twitter, while a score near negative 100 signifies a highly infamous search query. A score near 0 means that the query is not popular on Twitter.
